About

Hunter Wilkins is a concept artist, musician, singer, writer, and programmer.

His artwork focuses on themes of fantasy, science fiction, romance, and religion. Most of his pieces are made digitally with a combination of Adobe Photoshop and a Wacom tablet. Hunter's style heavily emphasizes believability, immersion, and entertainment over realism or political meaning.
